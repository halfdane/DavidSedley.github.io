Bev Sedley

Bev Sedley retired in November 2002 as co-ordinator of Lifecraft, a Cambridge mental health voluntary organisation which she founded and ran for its first ten years, subsequently returning as chair of the Board of Trustees. She now works largely as a Trustee of Cambridge Carbon Footprint and Chair of Cambridge Sustainable Food.

She has some dietary requirements.
